# Review Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing a draft in `_drafts/`.

## Content

- Does the draft say one clear thing?
- Is the scope bounded?
- Are the sources present?
- Does it belong in canonical folders, or should it stay a draft?

## Structure

- Is the frontmatter complete enough?
- Is `primary_domain` set?
- Is `review_state` correct?
- Are there obvious link targets that should be added?

## Decision

- `approved` when the draft is ready for promotion
- `rejected` when the draft needs actionable revision
- `blocked` when an external dependency prevents progress
