# Problem

Without a clear standard, Canonical Knowledge becomes a mix of drafts, notes, opinions, and oversized summaries.

# Claim

Canonical Knowledge should be concise, bounded, sourced, and reusable.

# Evidence

- Over-broad notes are difficult to maintain and easy to misuse.
- Unsourced notes drift into folklore.
- Multi-agent environments need a stable factual layer.

# Applicability

Use this standard for notes promoted into `wiki/`.

# Non-goals

- This is not a standard for raw source capture.
- This is not a standard for temporary brainstorming.
