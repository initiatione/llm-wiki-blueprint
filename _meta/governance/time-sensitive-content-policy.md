# Problem

Time-sensitive information becomes misleading when absorbed into evergreen notes without dates and boundaries.

# Claim

Recency-dependent content belongs in `news/` until it is intentionally distilled into stable knowledge.

# Evidence

Use `news/` for:

- AI releases and ecosystem news
- investment and market developments
- regulation, policy, and standards updates

Every time-sensitive note should include:

- explicit date or `as_of`
- sources
- why it matters
- what part is likely to age

# Applicability

Use this policy whenever a note depends on time, version, market state, or external events.

# Non-goals

- Not every dated note belongs in `news/`.
- Stable principles extracted from news can still live in Canonical Knowledge.
